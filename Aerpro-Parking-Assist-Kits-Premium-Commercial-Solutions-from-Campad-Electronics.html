Installation Highlights

Wired Kits: Connect the ECU to the vehicle’s power supply and route the sensor cables along the bumper. The beeper plugs directly into the ECU, providing instant audible alerts.

Wireless Kits: Mount the ECU in the engine bay (front) or externally (rear), pair with the LCD display or beeper and affix the sensors using the supplied brackets. No cable routing through the firewall is required, reducing installation time dramatically.

Paintable Sensors: All sensors come in matte black and can be painted to match the vehicle’s finish, preserving factory aesthetics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Aerpro kits are available for rear parking sensors and reverse parking sensors?
Campad Electronics stocks the wired APPS4R rear sensor kit and the wireless APPS4WCR rear kit (ideal for trucks, trailers and caravans). Both provide four ultrasonic rear bumper sensors, a beeper and ECU and are covered by a 5-year warranty.
Can I install a backup sensor kit myself and are the kits marketed as DIY/self-install parking sensor kits?
Yes. All Aerpro kits are supplied as self-install parking sensor kits with step-by-step guides. The wired kit requires routing a single cable, while the wireless kits need only sensor mounting and ECU pairing.

Use the “Contact Us” option on the Campad Electronics website or search “rear parking sensors fitted near me”. The tool lists authorized dealers and installers across Australia and offers nationwide shipping.
Are Aerpro rear sensor for car kits compatible with both plastic and metal bumpers?
Yes. All sensors are matte-black, paintable and come with universal brackets that fit plastic or metal bumpers, as well as under-tray mounting options.
What detection range and accuracy do the backup sensors provide?
Each ultrasonic sensor covers 0.2m–2.5m (rear) or 0.3m–2.5m (front). Audio alerts increase in frequency as an obstacle approaches, giving precise distance feedback for car reverse parking sensor applications.
Can Aerpro parking sensors be combined with a backup camera with sensors or a backup camera with parking sensors?
Yes. The optional wireless LCD display (APPSDD) accepts an auxiliary video input, allowing a reverse camera feed to be shown alongside the sensor distance read-out for a complete parking-assist system.
What is the wireless range for the back up sensor system on the APPS4WCR kit?
The APPS4WCR wireless rear kit provides up to 60m of ECU-to-beeper range, ideal for long trailers and commercial vehicles.
How does GPS speed control work on the wireless front and rear kits?
The GPS module automatically reduces beeper volume and alert frequency at higher speeds, while increasing sensitivity when the vehicle slows down, preventing false warnings during normal driving.
Which Aerpro kit is best for a back up sensor kit on trucks or trailers?
The APPS4WCR wireless rear kit is designed for trucks, vans, buses, caravans and horse floats. Its IP67-rated external ECU and 60m range make it the most robust back up sensor kit for heavy-duty use.
What warranty and support does Campad Electronics provide for backup sensors and backup sensors kit purchases?
All Aerpro kits come with a 5-year warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Campad Electronics offers phone, email and live-chat technical support, plus access to authorized service centres for warranty claims.
Are there accessories to improve visual feedback for car backup sensor kits?
Yes. The optional APPSDD wireless LCD display adds a clear visual read-out of distance, battery voltage and, when paired with a reverse camera, a live video feed, enhancing the car backup sensor experience.
How do I choose between a wired back up sensor kit and a wireless backup proximity sensor system?
Choose the wired APPS4R if you prefer a lower-cost solution and have easy access to run a single cable to the ECU. Opt for a wireless backup proximity sensor system (APPS4WF, APPS4WFR or APPS4WCR) for a cleaner install, longer sensor-to-ECU distances and the ability to fit on trailers or vehicles without drilling through the firewall.
